FAQS

What types of mortgages do you offer?

At Desh Capital LLC, we offer a variety of mortgage types, including fixed-rate mortgages, adjustable-rate mortgages (ARMs), FHA loans, jumbo loans, and more. We can help you find the mortgage that best fits your needs and financial situation.

Is Desh Capital LLC licensed and insured?

Yes, Desh Capital LLC is licensed and insured to provide mortgage services. Our team is dedicated to maintaining compliance with all relevant laws and regulations.

How much can I borrow for a mortgage?

The amount you can borrow for a mortgage depends on a number of factors, including your income, credit score, debt-to-income ratio, and the value of the property you are looking to purchase. Our team can work with you to determine your borrowing power and provide you with a pre-approval letter so you can start shopping for homes.

How long does it take to get a mortgage?

The timeline for getting a mortgage can vary depending on a number of factors, including the type of mortgage, your financial situation, and the current state of the housing market. In general, the process can take anywhere from 30 to 60 days, but our team will work to make the process as efficient and streamlined as possible.

Can I refinance my mortgage with Desh Capital LLC?

Yes, we offer refinancing options for homeowners who are looking to lower their monthly mortgage payments, shorten their loan term, or access their home equity. Our team can help you determine whether refinancing is the right choice for your financial goals and guide you through the process.

What if I have bad credit? Can I still get a mortgage?

While having a good credit score can help you qualify for more favorable mortgage terms, we understand that not everyone has perfect credit. Our team will work with you to assess your financial situation and explore mortgage options that may be available to you, including FHA loans and other government-backed programs.

How can I contact Desh Capital LLC for more information?

You can contact Desh Capital LLC by visiting our website and filling out a contact form, or by calling us directly at our listed phone number. Our team is happy to answer any questions you may have about our mortgage products and services.
